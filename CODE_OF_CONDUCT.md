# Code Of Conduct

BharatFlow AI should be a useful, respectful place for contributors who want to build serious workflow automation for India's businesses.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct, kind, and specific.
- Assume good intent while still naming technical risks clearly.
- Keep issues and pull requests focused on the work.
- Welcome new contributors and explain context when it is missing.
- Respect privacy. Do not share customer data, private screenshots, secrets, or personal information.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, insults, threats, or sustained hostile conduct.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Spam, low-effort promotional comments, or unrelated solicitation.
- Encouraging unsafe security disclosure or abuse of vulnerabilities.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns to [anishraj.dev@gmail.com](mailto:anishraj.dev@gmail.com). Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, block contributors, or take other reasonable action to keep the project healthy.
